About SHWAAS

SHWAAS detects human breath in disaster zones using cutting-edge sensors and AI. It connects to rescue teams worldwide via satellite and cloud, ensuring no life is left undiscovered.

Our Technology

  • Multi-sensor breath detection (humidity, CO₂, thermal imaging)
  • AI breath pattern recognition
  • Works in smoke, floodwater, and rubble
  • Real-time geo-tagged alerts
